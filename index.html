About

In an age where worship has become entertainment and theology has been replaced by experience, Reverb Conference exists to call the church back to biblical, God-centered worship.

"Biblical worship that echoes the glory of God."

We equip worship leaders, musicians, tech teams, and pastors with sound doctrine and faithful practice. Grounded in the Regulative Principle, we help churches worship in a way that honors Scripture, exalts Christ, and glorifies God alone.
